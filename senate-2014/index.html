Methodology: The combined percentage represents the Democratic Party Candidate's chance of winning the Seanate seat for each state. For organizations providing qualitatative forecasts, 'Likely Dem.' is coded as 90%, 'Leaning Dem.' as 75%, 'Tossup' as 50%, 'Leaning Rep.' as 25%, and 'Likely Rep.' as 10%. The Rothenberg Political Report includes two additional categories: "Toss-up/Tilt Democrat" is coded as 60%, "Toss-up/Tilt Republican" is coded as 40%.
